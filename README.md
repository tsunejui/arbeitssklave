# Arbeitssklave

> AI-powered workflow assistant that takes the grind out of daily standups and status reports for tech workers.

## Overview

**Arbeitssklave** is a project built to simplify the daily routine meetings and reporting workflows that consume so much of a tech worker's time. Instead of manually gathering context from a dozen tools, copy-pasting snippets into a daily report, and reciting the same updates in every standup, Arbeitssklave uses AI to do it for you.

The goal is simple: let engineers focus on building, and let AI handle the reporting overhead.

## What It Does

Arbeitssklave connects to the tools you already use at work, gathers your daily activity, and uses AI to produce a concise, supervisor-ready summary of what you accomplished and what's next.

- **Integrates with work software** — pulls activity from the tools where your real work happens (issue trackers, code hosts, calendars, chat, docs, etc.).
- **Consolidates daily activity** — aggregates scattered signals into a coherent picture of what you worked on.
- **AI-generated daily reports** — turns raw activity into a clean, human-readable summary suitable for standups or written status reports.
- **Supervisor-friendly output** — produces reports in the tone, format, and level of detail your manager actually wants to read.

## Who It's For

- Software engineers, data scientists, and other tech workers who attend daily standups or send daily/weekly reports.
- Team leads who want to reduce the reporting friction across their team.
- Anyone who has ever stared at an empty "what did you do yesterday?" box at 9:59 AM.

## Why "Arbeitssklave"?

*Arbeitssklave* is German for "work slave" — a tongue-in-cheek nod to the repetitive reporting grind this project aims to eliminate. Let the AI be the arbeitssklave, so you don't have to.
